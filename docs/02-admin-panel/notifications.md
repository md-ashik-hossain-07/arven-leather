# ARVEN Commerce OS (ACOS)

# Notifications

Version: 2.0

Status: Completed

---

# Purpose

The Notification Center delivers real-time alerts, reminders, approvals, and operational updates across the platform.

Notifications should ensure employees never miss important business events.

---

# Scope

- System Notifications
- Order Notifications
- Customer Notifications
- Inventory Alerts
- Marketing Notifications
- Employee Notifications
- Security Alerts

---

# Notification Types

Supported channels:

- In-App
- Email
- SMS
- WhatsApp
- Push Notification

Future channels may be added.

---

# Notification Categories

Examples:

- New Order
- Payment Received
- Order Cancelled
- Refund Requested
- Inventory Low
- Product Out of Stock
- Customer Registered
- Campaign Started
- Employee Assigned
- System Alert

---

# Priority Levels

Notifications support:

- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

Critical notifications require immediate attention.

---

# Notification Center

Display:

- Title
- Description
- Category
- Priority
- Date
- Status
- Read / Unread

Employees may search and filter notifications.

---

# Automation

Notifications may be triggered by:

- Order Events
- Customer Events
- Inventory Events
- Marketing Events
- Security Events
- Scheduled Events

# Delivery Tracking

Track:

- Sent
- Delivered

- Failed
